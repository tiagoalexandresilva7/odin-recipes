Spaghetti alla Carbonnara

Description

This mouth-watering recipe is ready in just 30 minutes and the ingredients detailed below can serve up to 4 people.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Black Pepper
  • Cheese
  • ABSOLUTELY NO CREAM
  • Pancetta or Bacon
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil Spaghetti
  2. Beat Eggs, add Black Pepper and Cheese
  3. Saute Pancetta (or Bacon) for 5 minutes
  4. Add Spaghetti and Saute for 3 more minutes
  5. Turn off the heat then add the Egg mixture
  6. Mix and grate extra Pecorino Romano on top
