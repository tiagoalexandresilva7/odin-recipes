Buffalo Chicken Wings

Description

Try this hot wings recipe for Buffalo chicken wings similiar to those served at a popular restaurant chain. If you have ever had them, you will love them.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Chicken Wings
  • Oil and Butter
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Whisk Flour, Paprika, Cayenne Pepper and Salt together
  2. Coat Chicken Wings with it
  3. While Oil is heating, combine Butter, Hot Sauce, Pepper and Garlic Powder on low heat until blended
  4. Fry Chicken Wings for 10 to 15 minutes
  5. Mix the Chicken Wings and the blend mixture in a bowl
